    Software Engineer

    Software engineers are experts in the testing, design, creation, and upkeep of computer software for both business and personal usage. They create management applications using mathematical, engineering, and computer science concepts. These individuals are in charge of programming machines to ensure that they function properly.

    Entrepreneur/Stock Inverstor

    Entrepreneurs are in charge of organizing and directing a company's activities. They also formulate strategies, set targets, and meet with potential clients and investors. Entrepreneurs who deal with day-to-day activities often recruit employees and plan work schedules. They may also be in charge of all aspects of the company's goods and services, such as marketing, distribution, and sales. Entrepreneurs examine financial statements to see if they can boost earnings and cut costs. They are also in charge of deciding the company's potential course.
